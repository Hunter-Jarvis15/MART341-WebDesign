About Sendak

Best known for his children’s books, WHERE THE WILD THINGS ARE and IN THE NIGHT KITCHEN,
Maurice Sendak has spent the past fifty years bringing to life a world of fantasy and imagination.
His unique vision is loved around the globe by both young and old. Beyond his award-winning work
as a writer and illustrator of children’s books, Sendak has produced both operas and ballets for
television and the stage.
